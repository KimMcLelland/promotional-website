My creative writing

Current Project:

A contemporary fantasy series featuring five characters with extraordinary powers struggling to find their place in the world and fighting against various tyrannical, cruel, manipulative or supernatural enemies.

The heroes:

  • A depressed alcoholic chosen by the pagan gods and wielding powers of the elements
  • A survivor of emotional and mental abuse, who accidentally consumes a holy relic and gains the power of healing
  • A non-binary trans woman who can shapeshift into any form but whatever form she takes she always has the same scar
  • A woman, another abuse survivor, who lost her sight but designed a cybernetic replacement for her eyes, only for them to malfunction and allow her to tap into a whole world's worth of knowledge and to see through lies
  • An intelligent young woman who embraced Egyptian mythology in an attempt to connect with her african heritage and then goes on to learn ancient magical arts

The enemies they face range from abusers, bigots, religious hypocrites and capitalist overlords to more supernatural threats such as a powerful but emotionally damaged necromancer, demonic entities, ghosts, dragons and strange beings from a future universe actively working to speed up the collapse of our universe.

Themes explored include sexuality, gender, spirituality, abuse, capitalism and the inevitability of our own mortality.
