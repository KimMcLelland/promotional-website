My media studies and experience

I am educated to Masters level. My adult education began in 2000 when I studied History and Philosophy at Manchester Metropolitan University and got a 2.1

But my media studies began years later than that. I studied BTec Multimedia Design from 2006 to 2008 and learnt many aspects of filming, photography, photoshop and audio and video editing there.

Following that I studied my Masters in Multimedia Journalism, graduating in 2019. This involved written journalism but also broadcast journalism, building on those filming and editing skills. For my disseration I made an audio documentary about Paganism and the LGBT Community.
